Set "Emissive" and "Display" material.

Parameters
emissivityEmissivity between 0.0f to 1.0f, being 1.0f full emissive.
emissivePartColor"Emissive" color, multiplied with diffuse texture.
displayPartColor"Display" color, multiplied with diffuse texture.

Both materials are affected by emissivity, since Keen never uses "Display" material they may remove it in the future, use it at your own risk.
